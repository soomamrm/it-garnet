EXPERIENCE OUR HISTORY

The Buffalo River, just one of Arkansas' many rivers, is home to some of the most fun and unforgettable adventures. Flowing 135 miles through the Ozarks with many wildlife calling it their home. The Ozarks are protected by the National Parks Service. There are many different types of wildlife that live in the area as well. You can see deer, black bear, eagle, many different types of fish, other bird species, and the bountiful Arkansas Elk Herd. With so many activities to explore, there is something for everyone of all ages. Hike thought of some of the most breathtaking views, experiencing nature for yourself. Canoe down the river and see many of the wildlife that inhabit the Ozarks. Take a chance and leave the city behind!
