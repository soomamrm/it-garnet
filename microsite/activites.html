Activities at the Great Smoky Mountains

Hiking

Explore the numerous trails and enjoy the breathtaking scenery while hiking through the Great Smoky Mountains.

Camping

Experience the beauty of nature by camping at one of the designated campgrounds in the Great Smoky Mountains National Park.

Wildlife Viewing

Observe a variety of wildlife, including black bears, deer, and birds, in their natural habitat.
